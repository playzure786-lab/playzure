Welcome to Playzure Studio (“we,” “us,” or “our”). We respect your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. Manage permissions:
Android: Settings → Apps → [Game] → Permissions
iOS: Settings → Privacy → [Permission Type] → [Game]
